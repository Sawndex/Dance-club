About APU Dance Club

We are a student-led club dedicated to fostering creativity, self-expression, and community through dance.

Our Mission

To make dance accessible to every student, regardless of skill level, by providing a safe and inclusive space to learn, practice, and perform.

Our Vision

To be the heartbeat of campus dance culture, known for vibrant events, inclusive workshops, and a community that values movement as a vital part of student life.

Our History

Founded in 2022 by a small group of students who wanted to share their love for dance, the club has grown into a diverse collective covering ballroom, Latin, hip-hop, contemporary, and cultural dance styles. We are now one of the largest student organisations on campus.

What We Do

  • Organise weekly dance workshops in various styles.
  • Host social dance nights and performances.
  • Provide a platform for members to showcase their talent.
  • Build a supportive community for all dance enthusiasts.
