Resources for Dancers

Helpful guides, dancewear tips, and resources to support your dance journey.

Essential Dancewear (Beginners)

  • Comfortable clothes that allow full range of motion
  • Dance shoes (depending on style – sneakers, jazz shoes, or dance heels)
  • Water bottle to stay hydrated
  • Small towel for sweat
  • Positive attitude and open mind!

Dance Style Quick Guide

  • Salsa: Latin dance with a fast tempo, 8-count timing, intricate turns
  • Bachata: Romantic Latin dance, 4-count timing, smooth hip movement
  • Hip-Hop: Street dance style with bounces, isolations, and grooves
  • Contemporary: Expressive style with fluid movements and emotional depth
  • Ballroom: Partner dances like Waltz, Tango, Foxtrot with elegance and precision

Dance Safety Tips

  • Always warm up before dancing to prevent injuries.
  • Stay hydrated – take water breaks regularly.
  • Listen to your body – don't push through pain.
  • Wear proper footwear for each dance style.
  • Practice on a safe, clear surface.
