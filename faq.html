Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining our dance community.

Do I need to be an experienced dancer to join?

Not at all! We welcome complete beginners and professional dancers alike. Our workshops are designed for all skill levels.

Do I need a partner to join?

No partner required! For partner dances like salsa and bachata, we rotate partners during class so everyone gets to practice.

What should I wear to a workshop?

Comfortable clothes you can move in. For shoes – sneakers for hip-hop, jazz shoes for contemporary, or dance heels for Latin styles.

Can I join if I'm not an APU student?

Unfortunately, membership is currently limited to APU students. However, you can still attend public events like social dance nights.

How often do you hold workshops?

We hold at least two workshops per week, covering different dance styles. Check our Events page for the latest schedule.

How much does membership cost?

RM 20 per academic year. See our Membership page for details and sign-up.
